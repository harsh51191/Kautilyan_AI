    Forecast cycle: from 7–8 days to same-day brief

    Manufacturing company · 500+ employees · Group IT Head

    The situation

    The system of record had the numbers. Market context lived in WhatsApp, email, and field judgment. Two or three people coordinated inputs from dealers, CRM, ERP, and finance every month before leadership had a usable forecast.

    The operating tax

    7–8 days of monthly coordination before leadership could act on a consolidated forecast - with variance explanations assembled manually each cycle.

    What Kautilyan did

    • Stage 0: Mapped the real forecast workflow - handoffs, missing inputs, and decision points.
    • Stage 1: Redesigned the workflow for daily signal assembly and human-approved briefs.
    • Stage 2: Pilot on one business unit with a named metric: same-day operating brief vs. multi-day cycle.

    Outcome

    The forecast preparation cycle that used to take eight days now generates a same-day operating brief - with governance and audit trail intact.
