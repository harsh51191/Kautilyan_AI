What your result band means

After you submit the questionnaire, you land in one of three bands. Each band includes a recommended next step toward execution — not a label for its own sake.

Low risk

Signals suggest limited structural drain relative to your stage — often still worth validating with numbers. Most founders who score low discover a medium-risk situation within the first 15 minutes of Stage 0 — because the cost is in the specific numbers, not the general sense.

Moderate risk

Multiple dependency or leakage signals show up — enough that waiting has a cost. This is the profile Stage 0 was built for. The rupee number typically surprises people.

High / critical risk

Severe concentration of knowledge, founder involvement, or operational fragility — material rupee exposure and execution risk. We recommend booking within 7 days. Harsha will personally review your answers before the call.
