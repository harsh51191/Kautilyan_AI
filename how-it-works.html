Our methodology

The SLOPE Framework

Five principles behind every engagement — not a sales process, a discipline.

Hover or click a card to read the full principle.

S

Start Small

One workflow. One metric. Proof before scale.

Every failed AI implementation tried to transform too much at once. One workflow with a named metric has a clear owner and definition of success.

Example: Monthly dealer forecasting only — Day-45 metric: cut prep from seven days to same-day.

L

Leverage Right Tools

Architecture before models.

Prompts, playbooks, tools, hooks, agents, and memory — each in the right place for the outcome.

Example: CRM + ERP read-only hooks and one approval playbook — not a chatbot on WhatsApp.

O

Own Your Data

Your keys. Your systems. Your governance.

BYOK with Gemini, Claude, or OpenAI. Read-only access and data minimisation. Never used to train our models.

Example: Client keys in their cloud; every agent read and approval is logged.

P

Powered Experiments

45-day pilots with named metrics.

Named outcome before we start. Evidence at Day 45. Refund if we miss the agreed outcome.

Example: Leadership reporting pilot — 40% less manual reconciliation or refund.

E

Evaluate Always

Outcomes, not activity.

Approval rates, time saved, and decision quality — not tokens used or agents deployed.

Example: Track approval cycle time before expanding autonomy.

The smallest safe architecture that achieves the business outcome.
