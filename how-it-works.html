Our methodology

The SLOPE Framework

Five principles behind every engagement — not a sales process, a discipline.

S

Start Small

One workflow. One metric. Proof before scale.

Every failed AI implementation tried to transform too much at once. A single workflow with a named metric has a clear owner, timeline, and definition of success.

L

Leverage Right Tools

Architecture before models.

Prompts, playbooks, tools, hooks, agents, workflows, memory — each in the right place. The smallest safe architecture for the outcome.

O

Own Your Data

Your keys. Your systems. Your governance.

BYOK with Gemini, Claude, or OpenAI. Read-only access, data minimisation, permission control. Never used to train our models.

P

Powered Experiments

45-day pilots with named metrics.

Named outcome before we start. Evidence at Day 45. Refund guarantee if we miss the agreed outcome after dependencies are met.

E

Evaluate Always

Outcomes, not activity.

Approval rates, exception patterns, time saved, decision quality — not tokens used or agents deployed.

The smallest safe architecture that achieves the business outcome.
